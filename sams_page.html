About Us


The Blank Project was started in 2017 in Miami by four local women. This program is commited to aid women in finding a career. At The Blank Project, we connect women seeking employement with employers in a variety of buisnesses. We help guide each client with the best profession, focusing on their strengths and skill set.

Why?

We started this program because of the need in our community to increase employment oppertunities for women. There is a large number of women who can not support themselves or their family because of the lack of employment. We believe that women should have the privilage to work in jobs where they are not discriminated. Our goal is to empower women around the globe to believe that they are powerful and capable of being successful in a career.

Our Team

The team of women who founded The Blank Project are participating in Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program 2017. We all agree that there needs to be an improvement with women in the work place. Each of the members on our team is passionate about this cause and how this program can help women in the future.
